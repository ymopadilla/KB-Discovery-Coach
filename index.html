About Dr. Kim

Guiding Leaders Through The Messy Middle

I am Dr. Kim Bland, a Discovery Coach dedicated to helping individuals navigate life's most challenging transitions. With nearly a decade of experience coaching and training leaders across multiple industries, I've walked alongside countless people experiencing burnout, identity transitions, and the disorienting uncertainty that comes when external success no longer aligns with internal clarity.

My approach is grounded in reflection, guided questions, and proven frameworks. I help people discover self, give them language for their experience, build confidence through new awareness, and ask powerful questions that reveal gaps between where they are and where they're meant to be. It's about what is discovered—and more importantly, how you'll apply it.

Everything I do is connected to a framework I've developed called Face It. Name It. Live It.—a structured process that moves people from awareness to meaning-making to aligned action.

Signature Offering

The Discovery Map Retreat

The Discovery Map is a transformative two-day retreat designed for individuals navigating burnout, transition, or a loss of identity. It's not a passive getaway—it's an active, structured journey that bridges the gap between where you are and where you're meant to be.

Through guided reflection, experiential exercises, and supportive community, participants move from feeling stuck and confused to feeling alive, aligned, and equipped with a tangible plan forward.
